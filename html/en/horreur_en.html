Horror

Horror is something I really enjoy, you know? It's like being on an emotional rollercoaster, where every thrill becomes a personal moment. These horror films or books, they have this power to startle you, but in a totally captivating way. It's as if each story is an adventure in which you take part, creating personal links with the unknown. And with horror video games, it's downright like taking control of your own scary experience. It's a unique sensation every time, a kind of personal thrill that you choose to face for that adrenaline rush.
